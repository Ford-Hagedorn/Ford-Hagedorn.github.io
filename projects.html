Some Projects I've Worked On:

I've worked on a number of projects while attending DevCodeCamp utilizing C#.

Some of them include:

FizzBuzz: A small program that counts from 1 to 100 in which multiples of 3 are replaced with the word "Fizz," multiples of 5 are replaced with "Buzz," and multiples of both 3 and 5 are replaced with "FizzBuzz."

Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock: A slightly more complicated game of Rock Paper Scissors, which allows for a player to compete with either another player or computer player.

Robots Vs. Dinosaurs: A game in which a fleet of 3 robots and a herd of 3 dinosaurs battle resulting in the victory of one team.

War Dice Game: A game where the user can input a number of sides for dice, then compare rolls to determine a winner.
